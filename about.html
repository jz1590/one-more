About Me

What moves me?

I love God and delight in studying about Him. Prayer and fasting are essential parts of my life. I love participating in an uplifting faith community where I am surrounded by people who help me to become more like God. I believe and bear testimony that Jesus Christ is the Son of God. That he was crucified to bear the sins of the world. That he has the power to redeem all men if they come to him and are baptized. Following the Gospel of Jesus is the path to happiness in this life and the next.

I enjoy playing and watching sports, especially with friends and family. I grew up playing a variety of sports and eventually decided to focus on volleyball where I had great success in high school. After high school, I decided to pivot out of the collegiate sports path and serve a mission in Brazil where I taught people about Jesus Christ. I then decided to advance my education at Brigham Young University where I am curerntly pursuing a degree in accounting.

Some things that I enjoy in my free time are the outdoors and travel. I also love to relax and have long conversations with friends and family, and I like watching movies and shows. Above all else, I love spending time with those I love most.
